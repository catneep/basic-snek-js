Snek

How to play

  • You control Snek, the friendly snake
  • Snek is represented by the green squares on the grid
  • Change the direction Snek is heading towards by using the arrow keys or WASD swiping on your screen
  • Snek grows larger after eating a yummy blue square
  • You get a point whenever Snek eats
  • Every once in a while, Snek will move faster, so stay frosty!
  • You can pause the game at any time by pressing 'space', 'esc' or 'enter' in your keyboard, or simply using the ⏯ button
  • Be careful, you'll lose if Snek hits a wall or eats itself
  • You win when Snek occupies the whole grid!

Good luck!
